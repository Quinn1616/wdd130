Idaho

Idaho is a northwestern state known for its mountains and rivers. Boise, Idaho is the state's capital with a population of 237,446 people. Idaho covers two time zones, runs from Canada to Nevada, and encompasses the western side of the continental divide of the Rocky Mountains. Rivers, mountains and farmland dominate the state's landscape.

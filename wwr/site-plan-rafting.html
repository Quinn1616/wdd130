Rafting Site Plan

Quinn Bastian

Overview

Purpose

To have an online presence that informs locals and visitors in the area about the fun of water rafting.

Audience

The target audience is males working a desk job, around 30 years old, stable finances, and looking for a weekend activity. They want to find an activity to help them be more adventurous. They expect to find how much water rafting costs and the locations. The audience will usually access the site through a mobile device.
